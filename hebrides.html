The Hebrides International Film Festival

The Hebrides International Film Festival (HIFF) is Scotland's leading island-based film event, held annually across the Outer Hebrides, including Lewis, Harris, Uist, and Barra. Founded to bring independent cinema to remote communities, HIFF showcases a curated selection of international dramas, documentaries, and short films, often themed around environmental issues and island life. Screenings take place in local venues and arts centres such as An Lanntair in Stornoway, complemented by Q&A sessions, masterclasses, and outdoor activities like wildlife walks and beach cleans. Recent editions have focused on climate action and sustainable rural living, offering audiences unique perspectives on global challenges while celebrating the cultural richness of the Hebrides.
