Hippfest

HippFest, formally known as the Hippodrome Silent Film Festival, is Scotland's first and only festival dedicated to silent cinema with live musical accompaniment. Held annually in March at the historic Hippodrome in Bo'ness - Scotland's oldest purpose-built cinema - the festival combines exceptional screenings of classic and rediscovered silent films with newly commissioned scores performed by world-class musicians. Renowned for its warm, inclusive atmosphere, HippFest attracts film enthusiasts, music lovers, and industry professionals from across the globe. Beyond its diverse programme of international silent films, the festival offers workshops, talks, behind-the-scenes tours, and special events, making it a unique celebration of cinema heritage and creativity in an iconic Art Deco setting.
