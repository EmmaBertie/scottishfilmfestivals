The Glasgow Film Festival

The Glasgow Film Festival (GFF), founded in 2005, has grown into one of the UK's most celebrated film events, known for its welcoming atmosphere and diverse programming. Held annually across various venues in Glasgow, with the historic Glasgow Film Theatre as its centerpiece, the festival showcases a rich mix of international cinema, Scottish premieres, and independent films.
